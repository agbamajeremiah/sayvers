GENERAL

Sayvers is a decentralised trading platform that empowers users around the world to make secure profitable trading decisions with the support of technology.
Sayvers drops the probability of losses by supporting the investment of users by performing automated trade with artificial intelligence.
In this way, your assets generate earnings on a daily basis, available for withdrawal at the click of a button.

Sayvers is unique because:
- All of your investments are controlled and traded by you, free from human influence.
- Your funds are placed on automated trade, maximising profitability and securing your assets.
- Sayvers grants you access to live markets, from anywhere in the world.

Unlike other investment platforms that trade on your behalf and promise a certain percentage profit, Sayvers empowers you to become your own investment banker. As your assets are placed on automated trade, you would be able to view profits accrued on every market trend.

INVESTMENTS

You can access the Sayvers platform by downloading the app on the Google Playstore. After registration, you can proceed to purchase a dollar asset of any amount that would be placed on automated trade, generating earnings.

You can purchase a dollar asset from as low as $1. You make the decision on how much to place on trade. Sayvers helps you make profit, no matter the amount.

Earnings on dollar assets are made from automated trade, as the algorithm profits from the market trends. The value of the earnings are visible from the user dashboard.

Assets are the funds deposited and placed on automated trade. Earnings are the sum of the daily profits earned from trades.
You can view your assets and earnings from your dashboard. The value of the earnings are updated on a daily basis directly underneath the individual assets.

The Sayvers platform is completely free. The payment platforms we partner with might charge you while purchasing dollar assets, but this amount is a token sum. A small percentage is also dedicated towards maintenance of the platform and its server requirements.

WITHDRAWALS

You can withdraw your assets as soon as your account is verified. Verification process takes roughly a week, but after that, withdrawals can be made instantly, at the click of a button.

A variety of options are available to make withdrawals.
Assets: You can choose to withdraw only your assets from live trades. Earnings would then be automatically converted to assets.
Earnings: You can choose to withdraw only your earnings. That way, your assets continue to be traded for profit.
Assets+Earnings: You can choose to withdraw your assets alongside all earnings.
